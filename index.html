About Me

Nice to meet you! I'm David - a human with lots of interests living in Portland, Maine. Currently, I am attending Coding Temple Bootcamp with a concentration in Python + Data Science. I also have a background in Film, Technology, and Sales.

I attended the University of Rhode Island with a double major in Film and Computer Science. After falling in love with programming, I decided to drop my art focus and move to the New England Insitute of Technology to study Software Engineering. There, I worked with languages like C++, Javascript, HTML, CSS, Python, and some database management.

Inevitably, job opportunities came knocking and I took them up before finishing out my academics. Since then, I have had a successful career in Tech Sales. My path has led me from individual sales positions, working directly with businesses to outfit them with the tech to help them succeed, to managing vast regions where I was overlooking 200+ employees.

For the past several years, I've been involved with an SF based tech startup, which has been an amazing experience. Seeing a product being fully realized by such a talented group of inidividuals has inspired me to finish growing my skills as a developer and join their ranks. Look forward to lots of projects to come on this site!
